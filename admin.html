Our Vision

Velvet was born from a simple belief: privacy should be effortless, and beauty should be standard. In a world where every messaging app demands your phone number, your email, your identity — Velvet asks for nothing but your desire to connect. A 5–7 digit code is all you need. No data harvesting. No profiling. No compromise.

Built by Velvet Pages, with years of effort poured into every pixel, every animation, every encrypted byte. Founded by Mayukh Sarkar, Velvet is not just an app — it's a philosophy.
